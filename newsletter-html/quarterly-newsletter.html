Upcoming Conferences
January 19-21: Peptalk

On January 19-21: Peptalk Virtual Conference & Expo was successfully held online by the Cambridge Healthtech Institute (CHI). The virtual conference covered topics including protein engineering & development, protein & antibody therapeutics, characterization & analytics, aggregation & impurities, process technologies & purification, and biotherapeutic expression & production. CAS presented a virtual booth and a conference report will be available soon by CAS volunteers.

December 14-16: Antibody Engineering & Therapeutics (AET)

On December 14-16, the Antibody Engineering & Therapeutics (AET) virtual meeting was successfully held online by the Antibody Society. This meeting covered the lasted antibody science, technologies, and partners needed to accelerate next generation antibodies towards commercial success. Some hot topics included: T cell receptors and cytokines; bispecific T cell engagers; responding to the COVID-19 pandemic with antibodies; and inducible activation of antibodies to enhance therapeutic index. Click below for a report prepared by CAS volunteers.

December 5, 2020: mTalks series “Revisit the Perspectives of Bispecific Antibodies under the COVID-19 Pandemic”virtual symposium

On December 5, the 2020 mTalks series “Revisit the Perspectives of Bispecific Antibodies under the COVID-19 Pandemic”virtual symposium was successfully held online by CAS. Ten speakers from both industry and academia shared their insights in topics of bispecific antibody-based therapeutics, including novel targets and molecular formats, COVID-19 related therapeutics, regulatory considerations, patent protection strategies, etc. Playback is open for CAS members.

Past Webinars
January 30: Challenges and insights of early phase clinical development in oncology

On January 30, Dr. Yaping Shou, Seasoned Clinical Drug Developer and former CMO of Trillium Therapeutics, introduced the principles and processes of early clinical development and unique features associated with the designs of oncology early phase trials. December 19: The Investment Outlook for Biotech in China

On December 19, Brad Loncar, CEO of Loncar Investments, discussed recent trends and catalyzing factors for the biotech sector in China, recent partnering deals and what they might signal, the IPO environment, and the overall investment landscape, etc. Click here for details.

Nov 20: Impact of CFIUS/FIRRMA and Export Control on Biotech Industry

On November 20, Catherin X. Pan-Giordano, Partner of Dorsey & Whitney LLP, talked about the final regulations implementing the Foreign Investment Risk Review Modernization Act (FIRRMA) of 2018. The new regulations expand the jurisdiction of the Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States (CFIUS) to review foreign investments. The new regulations have a significant impact on the biotech industry, as biotech companies raising funds and investors will need to carefully consider whether their transactions are subject to CFIUS review.
